Generation of dysarthic speech signals with
Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)

G. Manuguerra (2025)


This page contains some audio samples to evaluate the quality of synthetic dysarthric audio generation from healthy speech samples using a GAN. The Generator follows an image-to-image translation approach and consists of an initial encoding layer, a central body based on residual blocks in "sum" mode, and a final reconstruction layer. The discriminator is implemented as a deep convolutional classifier (CNN) and is tasked with classifying whether the input it receives is real or generated by the generator.

In listening to the following examples, keep in mind that Generator does not aim to precisely replicate the timbre of the target dysarthric speaker, but to imprint dysarthric characteristics onto a healthy speaker with a different vocal timbre.
